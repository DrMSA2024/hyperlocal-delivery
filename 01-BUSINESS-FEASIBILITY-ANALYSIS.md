## Revenue Model Feasibility

### Primary Stream - Delivery Commission
**Model:** 20% platform fee on delivery charge
- Delivery Fee: ₹30 (customer pays)
- Platform Revenue: ₹6
- Partner Payout: ₹24
- Target: 10,000 monthly deliveries = ₹60,000 MRR
- Feasibility: ✓ LOW RISK (standard industry model)
- Competitive: 40-50% lower than incumbents

### Secondary Streams

**Subscriptions (HIGH-MARGIN):**
- Daily Milk: ₹499/month = ₹30 per delivery (75% margin)
- Weekly Groceries: ₹899/month = ₹45 per delivery
- Target: 500 subscriptions = ₹3,50,000 MRR
- Feasibility: ✓ MEDIUM RISK (requires retention focus)

**B2B Premium:**
- Analytics Dashboard: ₹5,000-10,000/month
- Priority Placement: ₹2,000/month
- Target: 20-30 merchants = ₹1,50,000-2,00,000 MRR
- Feasibility: ✓ MEDIUM RISK (requires SMB sales)

**Surge Pricing:**
- Model: 1.5-2x multiplier during peak hours (5-7 PM)
- Volume: 20% of deliveries
- Impact: +₹60,000-80,000 monthly revenue
- Feasibility: ✓ LOW RISK

**Year 1 Revenue Mix:**
- Delivery Commission: 60% (₹22 Lakhs)
- Subscriptions: 25% (₹9 Lakhs)
- B2B Premium: 10% (₹3.6 Lakhs)
- Surge Pricing: 5% (₹1.8 Lakhs)
- **Total Year 1 Revenue:** ₹36.4 Lakhs
